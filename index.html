Services

I thrive to deliver custom-made analysis and interpretation solutions
for your biomedical big data.

Transcriptomics

Understanding cell phenotypes and regulation based on gene expression.

  • Single-cell or bulk transcriptomics
  • Spatial transcriptomics
  • Non-coding RNA

Epigenomics

Profiling DNA modifications to study gene regulation.

  • Chromatin accessibility (ATAC)
  • DNA-protein interactions (ChIP)
  • DNA methylation

Proteomics

Examining the proteome to decode cellular functions.

  • Mass cytometry / Flow cytometry
  • Mass spectometry

Immunomics

Analyzing immune system components, specificities and their interactions.

  • T/B cell receptor repetoire
  • Cytokine profiling

Multiomics

Integrating diverse 'omics' data for a comprehensive view.

  • Data integration
  • Meta analyses

Other

Custom bioinformatics for specialized or emerging fields.

  • Planning of OMICS experiments
  • Bioinformatics training
